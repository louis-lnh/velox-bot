Register Your Team

Registration for Season 1 of the Sideswipe Championship League (SCL) is open from 1 December to 28 December.

How to Register

Follow these steps to successfully enter your team:

  • Join the official SCL Discord server
  • Create a ticket in the #registration channel
  • Provide your team name, player list, and leader
  • Upload your team logo (optional but recommended)

A Toornament registration link will also be available soon. You will need to assign your roster on the Toornament platform once approved.

Requirements

  • Teams must consist of 2–4 players
  • Players must be available on weekend matchdays
  • All players must be part of the SCL Discord server
  • Only the team leader can submit final scores

If you have questions, contact our staff on Discord.
